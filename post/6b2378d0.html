复试英语问答

发表于2023-03-20|更新于2023-03-21
|阅读量:

Can you introduce your family ?

There are four members in my family,my parents, my grandmother and I. My parents are both workers. They work very hard and their responsible attitude towards work has a great influence on me. During my preparation for postgraduate entrance examination, My parents’ love and support have always been my power.
我家有四口人，我的父母、祖母和我。我的父母都是工人。他们工作非常努力，他们对工作负责任的态度对我有很大的影响。在我准备研究生入学考试期间，父母的爱和支持一直是我的力量。

Can you introduce your hometown ?

I come from Changde, a beautiful city located in the northwest of Hunan Province. It has a history of over 2000 years and a long cultural history. Changde has various specialties, such as turtle and beef flour, as well as scenic spots such as Liuye Lake. Welcome to Changde to visit.
我来自常德，一个位于湖南西北部的美丽城市，它有2000多年的历史和悠久的文化历史。常德有着各种各样的特产，比如甲鱼和牛肉粉，还有柳叶湖等景点，欢迎来常德游玩。

Can you introduce your university ?

My university is Changsha University of Technology, located in Changsha. It has beautiful scenery and beautiful environment, and I am very grateful to my university because I have learned a lot of knowledge and increased my horizons in the university.
我的大学是长沙理工大学，位于长沙。它有美丽的风景和优美的环境，我十分感谢我的大学，因为我在大学里学习了很多知识，也增长了见识。

What interests do you have and how much fun these have brought you ?

I like playing table tennis, through which I have made many good friends, and playing table tennis can release my pressure and make me feel happy.
我喜欢打乒乓球，通过这我结识到了很多好朋友，并且通过打乒乓球可以使我释放压力，使我心情愉快。

What is your favorite undergraduate course ?

During my undergraduate period, one of my favorite courses was the principle of computer composition. I have learned a lot from this course. It made me realize the operating principle of computers and realize the charm of computers.
在本科期间，我最喜欢的课程之一是计算机组成原理。我从这门课中学到了很多。它让我认识到了计算机的运行原理，体会到了计算机的魅力。
