复试英语问答

发表于2023-03-20|更新于2023-03-24
|阅读量:

一般问题

Can you introduce your family ?

There are four members in my family,my parents, my grandmother and I. My parents are both workers. They work very hard and their responsible attitude towards work has a great influence on me. During my preparation for postgraduate entrance examination, My parents’ love and support have always been my power.
我家有四口人，我的父母、祖母和我。我的父母都是工人。他们工作非常努力，他们对工作负责任的态度对我有很大的影响。在我准备研究生入学考试期间，父母的爱和支持一直是我的力量。

Can you introduce your hometown ?

I come from Changde, a beautiful city located in the northwest of Hunan Province. It has a history of over 2000 years and a long cultural history. Changde has various specialties, such as turtle and beef flour, as well as scenic spots such as Liuye Lake. Welcome to Changde to visit.
我来自常德，一个位于湖南西北部的美丽城市，它有2000多年的历史和悠久的文化历史。常德有着各种各样的特产，比如甲鱼和牛肉粉，还有柳叶湖等景点，欢迎来常德游玩。

Can you introduce your university ?

My university is Changsha University of Technology, located in Changsha. It has beautiful scenery and beautiful environment, and I am very grateful to my university because I have learned a lot of knowledge and increased my horizons in the university.
我的大学是长沙理工大学，位于长沙。它有美丽的风景和优美的环境，我十分感谢我的大学，因为我在大学里学习了很多知识，也增长了见识。

What is your study plan?

If I were enrolled into my ideal research field, there must be many things to learn for me. First of all, I hope I can have a better command of professional knowledge. Secondly, I plan to participate in some relevant projects to get more experiences. If possible, I will continue study for doctor degree. In a word, I am looking forward to making a solid foundation for my job in the future.
如果我进入了我理想的研究领域，我肯定有很多东西需要学习。首先，我希望我能更好地掌握专业知识。其次，我计划参加一些相关的项目，以获得更多的经验。如果可能的话，我将继续攻读博士学位。总之，我期待着为我未来的工作打下坚实的基础。

What interests do you have and how much fun these have brought you ?

I like playing table tennis, through which I have made many good friends, and playing table tennis can release my pressure and make me feel happy.
我喜欢打乒乓球，通过这我结识到了很多好朋友，并且通过打乒乓球可以使我释放压力，使我心情愉快。

Why choose East China Normal University ?

Because East China Normal University has a strong strength in the field of computer science, I believe that I can learn more knowledge here. In addition, I particularly like Shanghai and hope to develop in Shanghai in the future.
因为华东师范大学在计算机科学领域有极强的实力，我相信在这里我可以学习到更多的知识，另外我特别喜欢上海，希望以后能来上海发展。

What did you do in the past four years?

I spend a lot of time studying and completing some hardware and software projects. In my spare time, I often play table tennis and take active part in volunteer activities.
我花了很多时间研究和完成一些硬件和软件项目。在业余时间，我经常打乒乓球，并积极参加志愿者活动。

What is your greatest weakness/shortcoming/disadvantage ?

I think my weakness is that I am not firm and easy to change my mind, but I am working hard to change now, and once I have determined one thing, I must stick to it.
我认为我的缺点是不坚定，容易改变主意，不过我现在努力改变，确定了一件事情就要坚持到底。

If you fail this time, what will you do in the near future?

I will continue to study for postgraduate entrance examination next year and I still choose East China Normal University. Firstly, I have a great interest in my major , computer software. Secondly, this university has always been my dream and I will try my beat to make my dream come true.
明年我将继续攻读研究生入学考试，我仍然选择华东师范大学。首先，我对我的专业计算机软件很感兴趣。其次，这所大学一直是我的梦想，我会尽力实现我的梦想。

专业问题

What is your favorite undergraduate course ?

During my undergraduate period, one of my favorite courses was the principle of computer composition. I have learned a lot from this course. It made me realize the operating principle of computers and realize the charm of computers.
在本科期间，我最喜欢的课程之一是计算机组成原理。我从这门课中学到了很多。它让我认识到了计算机的运行原理，体会到了计算机的魅力。

Can you tell me something about your project?

I would like to talk about my graduation project.It is mainly about the design and implementation of a target tracking system, which is divided into two steps. The first step is to train the twin network model, and the second step is to use model calculations to achieve target tracking.
我想谈谈我的毕业设计。它主要是关于目标跟踪系统的设计与实现，分为两步，第一步是训练孪生网络模型，第二步是使用模型计算，实现目标跟踪。

Can you say something about your major?

My major is computer science and technology ,which includes the software and hardware of a computer , covering a wide range of fields and has a good development prospect.
我的专业是计算机科学与技术，包括计算机的软件和硬件，涵盖的领域很广，有很好的发展前景。

What is deep learning?

Deep learning is a research direction of machine learning, which aims to enable robots to learn and analyze data like humans.That’s my understanding. Thank you.
深度学习是机器学习的一个研究方向，旨在让机器人像人类一样学习和分析数据。这是我的理解。非常感谢。
